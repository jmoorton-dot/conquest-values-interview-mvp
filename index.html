Overview

Conquest Values Interview

This short interview captures your reflections across eight Conquest values. Your responses help the panel understand your working style and fit, alongside the rest of the interview process.

8 questions Approx 10 to 15 minutes Anonymous access for pilot Timezone Europe London

Before you begin

  • Answer in your own words, specific examples are best
  • You can take a moment to think before replying
  • Do not include confidential third party information

Consent

By starting this pilot interview, you consent to Conquest collecting and processing your responses for recruitment and evaluation purposes. AI may be used to assist with scoring and summarisation, but final decisions are made by humans.

Privacy

For this MVP, you will be asked for basic details such as your name and email so we can associate your responses to the role. Your responses are stored securely and are only accessible to authorised Conquest staff involved in the process. This page is unlisted and intended for invited participants only.
